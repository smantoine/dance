TWYLA THARP

“Creativity is Defiance”

Twyla Tharp still lives and works in New York City. Tharp formed her own company “Twyla Tharp Dance” in 1966. Tharp has integrated classical music, jazz and contemporary pop into her dance choreography.

In 1973, Tharp adapted the music of the Beach Boys for the Joffrey Ballet music. In 1971-1988 her dance company toured extensively worldwide. In 1973 Tharp choreographed Deuce Coupe to the music of the Beach Boys for the Joffrey Ballet.

Tharp has merged her background in classical ballet with her own view of expression. Tharp has choreographed dances for pre-eminent dance companies throughout the world.

In 2003, Tharp authored the book “The Creative Habit” which received serous attention and has been the basis for much critical thinking concerning Tharp’s career and her views of the formation of creativity.

She was born on July 1, 1941 in Portland, Oregon.

Tharp’s official website is: www.twylatharp.org.
