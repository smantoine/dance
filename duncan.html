ISADORA DUNCAN

“Dance is the movement of the universe concentrated in an individual.
You were once wild here...
Don't let them tame you”

Angela Isadora Duncan was an American dancer who rose to great prominence in Europe. Duncan was form in California but lived in Western Europe and the and the Soviet Union from the age of 22 until her untimely death at age 50 when Duncan in a freak accident when her scarf was caught underneath the car in which she was driving.

While Duncan, was a child of privilege, she absorbed and was inspired by folk dancing and atypical body movements. Her choreography demanded a recognition and acceptance of a new form of dance.

Duncan was born on May 27, 1877 in San Francisco and died in Niece France on September 14, 1927.
